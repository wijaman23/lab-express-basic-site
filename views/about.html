About

With so many people looking for a job, when a company posts a job offer, it can receive hundreds of CVs. Same template models, structures, data...

It is not surprising that when looking for a job, one of the keys is the differentiation and personalization of your application. A short and well-crafted cover letter can make a difference and put you one step ahead of the rest of the candidates.
